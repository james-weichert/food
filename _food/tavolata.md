I'm not usually one to eat pasta when going out (my homemade Bolognese is good enough for me), but I'll make an exception for to go to Tavolàta with friends. While the restaurant is generally a bit on the pricey side, if you're happy with pasta as your main course then $30 is not too bad all things considered. The foccacia and pappardelle ragu left me satisfied, and the atmosphere feels small and intimate. If you're looking for a little fancier spot for your next special occassion, this feels like a good bet.
