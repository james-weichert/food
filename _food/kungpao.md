[Originating](https://www.latimes.com/food/story/2019-11-07/kung-pao-chicken-history-recipe-gong-bao) in China's Sichuan province, Kung Pao chicken has also solidified its place in Chinese-American cuisine. I found it surprisingly easy to make (following [this recipe](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_QWXjebXiWk)), although I'm still **trying to perfect the balance of Shaoxing wine, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and MSG**.
