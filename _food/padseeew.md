After _finally_ getting around to buying a wok, I wanted to try my hand at making pad see ew, a Thai dish made with wide rice noodles and chinese broccoli that I've been ordering a lot recently. I used [this _NYT Cooking_ recipe](https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1027548-chicken-pad-see-ew) for the measurements and [this video tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JpPiLwuGmks) for the technique. I still need to perfect the sauce ratio to emulate the exact taste, but this dish was **surprisingly easy to put together**, provided you get your ingredients ready in advance (including soaking the rice noodles for 30+ minutes).

<strong class="tip">Tip!</strong> If you plan to make many Thai dishes, invest in some authentic Golden Mountain sauce, available at an Asian grocery store.
