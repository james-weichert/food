If you don't want to go through the (admittedly time-consuming) process of making your own bánh mì, I'd recommend [**Crackle Mi**](https://cracklemi.com/), with locations in Ballard and Fremont. The grilled pork bánh mì was **only $10 and had a very generous portion of meat**. Their fresh spring rolls are also great!

<strong class="tip">Seattle Adventure!</strong> Visit Crackle Mi before or after exploring **downtown Ballard**. Highlights of my recent weekend trip include the fish ladder at the [**Chittenden Locks**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballard_Locks) and a **free ticket to the [National Nordic Museum](https://nordicmuseum.org/)** through my **[Seattle library](https://www.spl.org/programs-and-services/arts-and-culture/museum-pass)** card.
