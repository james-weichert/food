Thịt kho is a Vietnamese staple consisting of **braised pork belly and hard-boiled eggs** which was pretty easy to make (and just what I needed at the end of the winter quarter). I followed [this recipe on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GE7qJ3cJlHw), which yielded a good first attempt (with lots of room for improvement, of course).
