When the weather turns cold and the sun sets early, it's time for me to get out my dutch oven and channel my inner Julia Child to make coq au vin (literally, 'chicken in wine'). The **basics involve searing chicken before deglazing and braising with red wine and stock along with carrots, onions, and mushrooms**. You can take your pick from among famous chefs' preparations of this classic dish when it comes to the details (see [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y47p2D5Z1l4) for a summary).
