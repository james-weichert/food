Among the many culinary masterpieces of Peruvian cooking, lomo saltado is simply unbeatable for me. **Stir fry thin strips of steak in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and aji amarillo paste** along with red onion, scallions, and tomatoes. Don't forget to serve with _both_ white rice and french fries!
